Sending a payment link is a very useful tool to guarantee client payment when selling through social media, chat, email, or over the phone. With Paymennt, you  can easily create and share a payment link with your customers.

## how to send a payment link

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="app" label="Mobile App" default>

### a) product payment link
use the **product** payment link to to generate an invoice from catalog products

1. tap the "+" button in the bottom right corner of mobile home page
2. select the type of link you wish to create: **product**
3. choose the products that your customer is buying
4. add an order ID and attach an invoice (optional)
5. select an existing customer or create a new one
6. select how you would like to share the link to your customer

### b) amount payment link
use the **amount** payment link to specify the amount you wish to collect

1. tap the "+" button in the bottom right corner of mobile home page
2. select the type of link you wish to create: **amount**
3. specify the amount required for the payment
4. add a description, order ID, and attach an invoice (optional)
5. select an existing customer or create a new one
6. select how you would like to share the link to your customer
