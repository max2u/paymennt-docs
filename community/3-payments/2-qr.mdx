For in-person payments, you can get paid using QR codes With Paymennt, you can easily create and share a QR code for in-person payments.

<br />
<br />

Open the Paymennt app homepage

1. Tap the "+" button in the bottom right corner of mobile home page
2. Specify the amount required for the payment
3. Add a description, order ID, and attach an invoice (optional)
4. Click on the QR code symbol in the list of payment options
5. The QR code is generated and displayed on the screen. Clients can scan and pay using their cards or apple pay.
